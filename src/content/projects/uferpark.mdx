## The starting point

A rare lakeside parcel near Zug, with the price expectation that comes with the
address — and a developer who needed the pre-sales to make the financing real.

## The chain

**Esad** reviewed feasibility and tuned the massing so that every apartment earns
its view. **Dionis** built a premium pricing strategy and a sales dossier to
match, then opened an off-plan reservation pipeline with renders, a brochure and a
microsite by **CloudOnPoint** — selling the lifestyle before a single floor was
poured.

## The outcome

Early reservations are tracking toward the quota the bank requires to release
construction credit, with the most exposed units moving first.
