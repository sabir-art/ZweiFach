## The starting point

A generous suburban plot with mature trees and a developer who wanted density
without losing the garden character that made the site attractive.

## The chain

**Esad** kept the building low and broke it into garden-facing volumes, designed in
a timber-hybrid system for speed and sustainability. **Dionis** used that garden
story as the spine of the positioning, opening a reservation pipeline off-plan
before the permit decision — so demand is being proven while the design is finalised.

## The outcome

The pre-sale campaign is live, tracking toward the quota the bank needs to release
construction credit. Renders and the reservation microsite are by **CloudOnPoint**.
