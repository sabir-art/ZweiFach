## The starting point

A steep, south-facing parcel that several parties had dismissed as too difficult
to develop economically.

## The chain

**Esad** treated the gradient as the brief, not the obstacle: six residences step
down the slope, each with a private terrace and an open view. The feasibility study
confirmed a scheme that is both buildable and financeable. **Dionis** has prepared
the pricing view and pre-sale strategy for the commercialization phase to come.

## The outcome

The project is in planning, with a clear, costed path from design to a pre-sale
campaign — the chain mapped before the first apartment is offered.
