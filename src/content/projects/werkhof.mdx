## The starting point

A disused workshop with good bones and an awkward question: keep the character and
make it pay, or knock it down. The owner wanted the first answer to work.

## The chain

**Esad** assessed the structure and designed nine lofts inside the existing shell,
keeping the steel windows and the volume that made the building worth saving.
**Dionis** made that character the selling point and pre-sold the lofts, with
renders and a brochure by **CloudOnPoint**.

## The outcome

The conversion sold through during construction and was handed over fully sold —
proof that the chain works on re-use, not just new build.
